System snapshot

Problem

Habitat decline is spatial, layered, and easy to miss.

Heat, water stress, air quality, and fragmentation rarely harm every species the same way. Most monitoring tools catch one signal, not the full ecological story.

Approach

We convert remote sensing into species-level evidence.

EcoScan combines satellite-style vegetation and moisture indicators with field sensors, then translates each habitat polygon into vulnerability signals for real species and plants.

Outcome

Teams can act on place, species, and intervention together.

Judges and field teams can see where the corridor is most fragile, which species are sounding the alarm, and which restoration move has the highest leverage.
